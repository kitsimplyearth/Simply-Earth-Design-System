Frequently Asked Questions

      

      

        

          

            What is the Recipe Box?
            
          

          
Each month you'll receive a box with 4 essential oils, a bonus big item, and all the supplies needed to make 6 full-size DIY natural products — right from your own home.

        

        

          

            How can I swap essential oils or other items?
            
          

          
You can customize your box before it ships! Log in to your account and visit the Box Customizer to swap out any items you'd prefer to replace.

        

        

          

            Can I add extra products to my Recipe Box?
            
          

          
Yes! You can add extra products to your order and they'll ship for free along with your Recipe Box.

        

        

          

            When will my Recipe Box ship each month?
            
          

          
Boxes ship between the 15th and 20th of each month. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email as soon as it's on its way!

        

        

          

            How does the #IMADETHIS monthly contest work and how can I win the $100 gift card?
            
          

          
Share a photo of your finished product on social media using #IMADETHIS. Each month we select a winner to receive a $100 Simply Earth gift card!

        

        

          

            I've unlocked the Hero Course — how do I share?
            
          

          
You can share your Hero Course progress by visiting your account dashboard and clicking the "Share My Journey" button to get your personal referral link.
